Presentation Tips & Guidelines
Timing: Allow 30-45 seconds per slide for natural pacing
Pauses: Stop reading to let key statistics and visuals resonate with the audience
Demo Focus: Spend extra time on Slide 5 if the iframe loads successfully - let audience interact
Questions: Pause at logical breaks (end of slides 2, 3, 6, 8) for audience questions
Navigation: Use arrow keys (← →) or spacebar to advance slides in the main presentation
Emphasis: Use hand gestures to point to key statistics, charts, and feature highlights
Print-Friendly: This page is optimized for printing with proper page breaks
Mobile-Friendly: Responsive design works on tablets and phones for on-the-go practice
